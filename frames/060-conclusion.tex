\subsection{General}
\begin{frame}
    \frametitle{Conclusion}
    \framesubtitle{General}

    \begin{itemize}
        \item<1->Intra-camera tracking well researched
              \vspace{5pt}
        \item<2->Inter-camera tracking still open research area with many challenges, especially in real-time scenarios
              \vspace{5pt}
        \item<3->Need for robust data association methods across cameras
              \vspace{5pt}
        \item<4->Edge computing could lead to more comprehensive and versatile systems
              \vspace{5pt}
        \item<5->Ethical and privacy concerns need to be considered in the development of MTMCT systems
              \vspace{5pt}
        \item<6->MTMCT will, as it already does, play an important role in many applications
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
